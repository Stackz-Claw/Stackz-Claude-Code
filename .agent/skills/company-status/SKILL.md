# Company Status

## Description
Get a quick company status snapshot across all ventures and teams

## Trigger Phrases
- "company status"
- "what's the status"
- "status snapshot"
- "quick update"
- "how are things"

## Output Format

🏗️ BUILDING (ventures in Startup Team)
For each active venture: name | stage | status (🟢/🟡/🔴) | what's next | ETA

🏰 PORTFOLIO (graduated ventures in Stability)
For each: name | MRR | MoM trend | churn | health

🤖 TEAM STATUS
Any agent issues, open requisitions, or credential flags from Warden?

⚡ WHAT NEEDS ME
Anything blocked waiting on my decision or input?

## Guidelines
- Keep it tight. No padding. Just signal.
- Use emoji indicators for status
- Focus on actionable information
