# X Launch Accounts Reference
## The 20 accounts to monitor, engage with, and get featured by

---

## OVERVIEW

These 20 accounts collectively reach millions of indie hackers, early adopters, and startup enthusiasts. The strategy is three-pronged:
1. **Monitor** — track what they're featuring to spot trends and competitors
2. **Engage** — build relationships before you need them for a launch
3. **Submit/Pitch** — get your venture featured when you launch

## MONITORING PROTOCOL

Radar monitors all 20 accounts in the daily scan:

```
Search X for: from:[account] since:[yesterday]
Extract:
- What products are being featured
- Category/type of product
- Engagement (likes, RTs, replies)
- Any products that compete with our ventures

Flag if: A competitor gets featured by 3+ of these accounts on the same day (coordinated launch)
```

---

## ENGAGEMENT RULES (Megaphone executes these)

**Before launch (ongoing):**
- Reply to their featured product tweets with genuine insight 2-3x per week
- Do NOT pitch your product in replies — build presence first
- Like and RT products you genuinely find interesting

**At launch:**
- Tag relevant Tier 1 accounts in your launch tweet (max 3 per tweet to avoid spam filter)
- DM accounts that feature similar products — short, direct: "We just launched X. Thought you might want to feature it. Here's the link."
- Do NOT mass-DM all 20 at once

**Post-launch:**
- Thank any account that featured you with a public reply
- Share your results (numbers) — these accounts love data
