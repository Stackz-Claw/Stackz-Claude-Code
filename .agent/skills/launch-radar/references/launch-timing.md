# Launch Timing Reference
## When to submit, how to sequence, and how to maximize momentum

---

## THE GOLDEN RULE

**Never launch everywhere on the same day.** Spread submissions over 2-4 weeks to maintain sustained traffic, freshness on each platform, and to use early platform traction as social proof for later ones.

---

## OPTIMAL SEQUENCE

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (2-3 weeks before)
**Goal:** Build waitlist, generate buzz, get feedback

Submit to:
1. **BetaList** — Submit 2-3 weeks early. "Upcoming" status builds waitlist.
2. **PeerPush** — Join community, start supporting others' launches now (earns reciprocal support)
3. **LaunchIgniter** — Get into the community early

---

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (1 week before main launch)
**Goal:** Get real users, testimonials, iron out bugs before the big day

Submit to:
4. **Indie Hackers** — Post your product + intro post in forum
5. **SideProjectors** — List product, gauge interest
6. **Peerlist** — Create project listing

---

### Phase 3: Main Launch Week (The Big Push)
**Goal:** Maximum visibility, user signups, press attention

**Day 1 (Tuesday preferred): Product Hunt**
- Launch at exactly 12:01am PT
- This is your highest-leverage day — have everything ready
- Announce on X immediately when live
- Use PeerPush connections for coordinated support

**Day 2: Hacker News Show HN**
- Post at 8-10am ET (while PH momentum is still visible)
- Different audience, different tone — write the technical story
- Link to PH in your HN text ("also launched on PH yesterday")

**Day 3: Uneed + Fazier**
- Submit same day — both are quick review
- Use PH upvote count as social proof in descriptions

**Day 4-5: Microlaunch + TinyLaunch + Tiny Startups**
- By now you have numbers to share — include them
- "Launched Tuesday, 400 upvotes on PH, 50 HN points, here's what we learned"

---

### Phase 4: Sustained Presence (Weeks 2-4)
**Goal:** Long tail traffic, community building

8. **Write Indie Hackers milestone post** — "We launched last week. Here's what happened."
9. **Post to relevant subreddits** (r/SaaS, r/microsaas, r/entrepreneur) — not launch platforms but amplifiers
10. **Re-engage** on platforms where you got comments — respond to everything

## DAY OF WEEK ANALYSIS

### Product Hunt
| Day | Performance | Reason |
|-----|-------------|--------|
| Tuesday | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Best | Full week attention, high staff/press activity |
| Wednesday | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Great | Strong audience, less competitive than Tue |
| Thursday | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Great | Good for B2B — buyers active |
| Monday | ⭐⭐⭐ OK | Some people not fully online yet |
| Friday | ⭐⭐ Weak | Weekend crowd-out starts |
| Weekend | ⭐ Avoid | Low engagement, less press coverage |

### Hacker News
| Day | Performance | Notes |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Tue–Thu | Best | Technical audience active, US morning ET |
| Monday | OK | Good engagement, slightly less competitive |
| Friday | Weak | Weekend brain starts |
| Weekend | Mixed | Can go viral but less sustainable |

### General Rule for All Other Platforms
Tuesday–Thursday, submit in the morning (platform's local time or UTC).

---

## TIME OF DAY

**Product Hunt:** Midnight PT sharp (12:01am) — the entire day runs midnight to midnight PT. Earlier = more time to accumulate votes.

**Hacker News:** 8–10am ET — catches US East Coast at work, gives time to build before US West wakes up.

**Everything else:** Morning submissions (9am–11am UTC) perform best — platforms curate their daily featured items mid-morning.

---

## MOMENTUM STACKING

Use each platform's results to fuel the next:

```
BetaList (pre-launch)
  → "200 people on our waitlist" (use in PH launch text)
  
Product Hunt launch
  → "We just launched on PH — X upvotes in Y hours"
  → Use this number in HN Show HN text
  → Tweet it tagged at all 20 X accounts
  
HN Show HN
  → "X points on HN" → use in Uneed/Fazier submissions
  → Screenshot the HN thread for social proof
  
All numbers combined
  → Week 2 Indie Hackers milestone post: "We launched. Here's the full breakdown."
  → This post often drives MORE signups than the original launch
```

## LAUNCH WEEK CHECKLIST

**T-7 days:**
- [ ] All platform accounts created and warmed up
- [ ] Submitted to BetaList (should already be live)
- [ ] PH scheduled / hunter confirmed
- [ ] PeerPush connections built
- [ ] All assets ready (logo, screenshots, descriptions per platform)
- [ ] X thread written and scheduled
- [ ] Email to existing waitlist drafted

**T-24 hours:**
- [ ] PH launch scheduled
- [ ] HN post drafted (don't submit yet)
- [ ] All platform descriptions finalized
- [ ] Ghost (marketing agent) ready with response templates

**T-0 (midnight PT):**
- [ ] PH goes live — confirm immediately
- [ ] First comment posted (founder story)
- [ ] X announcement posted
- [ ] Email blast to waitlist
- [ ] PeerPush network activated

**T+1h:**
- [ ] Check rank — are we climbing?
- [ ] Respond to all early comments
- [ ] Megaphone posts X update with early numbers

**T+24h:**
- [ ] HN Show HN post (now with PH numbers)
- [ ] Begin Tier 2 platform submissions
