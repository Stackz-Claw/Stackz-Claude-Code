## PRE-LAUNCH SOCIAL WARMUP (1-2 weeks before)

Before launch day, Megaphone establishes presence:

1. **Follow all 20 X accounts** (see `launch-radar/references/x-accounts.md`)
2. **Engage genuinely** — reply to 2-3 featured products per day with real insight
3. **Build in public** — 1 tweet per day about what you're building (no pitching)
4. **Join PeerPush** — start supporting other launches to earn reciprocal support

**Building-in-public tweet formats that work:**
- "Day [X] building [product]: [one thing you learned]"
- "Problem I'm trying to solve: [problem]. Here's my current approach: [approach]. Anyone dealt with this?"
- "Just hit [small milestone]. Here's what's working / not working."

## X LAUNCH THREAD

Structure every launch thread with this format. Megaphone drafts, Stackz approves.

```
TWEET 1 — THE HOOK (must stop the scroll):
🚀 We just launched [PRODUCT] on @ProductHunt!

[PRODUCT] [does specific thing] for [specific person].

No [pain point A]. No [pain point B]. Just [core value prop].

👇 Here's the story.
[PH link]

---

TWEET 2 — THE PROBLEM (make them feel it):
The problem we're solving:

[Specific person] spends [X hours/$ amount] [doing painful thing].

The tools that exist either:
❌ [Flaw of competitor A]
❌ [Flaw of competitor B]
❌ [Flaw of competitor C]

So we built something different.

---

TWEET 3 — THE PRODUCT (show, don't tell):
[PRODUCT] [does thing] by [how it works simply].

Key things it does:
→ [Feature 1 as benefit]
→ [Feature 2 as benefit]  
→ [Feature 3 as benefit]

[Screenshot or GIF here]

---

TWEET 4 — THE PROOF (if you have it):
Early results from beta users:
• [User type] saving [X hours/$/outcome]
• [Metric if available]
• "[Short testimonial quote]" — @user

[OR if no beta users yet:]
Built this because [personal story — why you needed this yourself].

---

TWEET 5 — THE TRACTION (after first few hours):
[POST AFTER T+2h WITH REAL NUMBERS]

We're X hours in:
🔥 [N] upvotes on @ProductHunt
⭐ Currently ranked #[X] today

If you haven't checked it out yet: [link]

Genuinely appreciate every single person who's tried it.

---

TWEET 6 — THE ASK (humble, direct):
If [PRODUCT] sounds useful:

→ Check it out: [product URL]
→ Support us on @ProductHunt: [PH link]
→ RT this thread if you know someone who'd find it useful

We read and respond to every reply. 🙏

[Tag selectively: @IndieHackers @BetaList — max 2 per tweet]
```

**Timing:** Post tweet 1 at midnight PT (same as PH launch). Post tweets 2-4 every 15 minutes. Tweet 5 at T+2h. Tweet 6 at T+4h.

## HACKER NEWS POST

HN requires a completely different voice than X. Technical, honest, no hype.

```
Title: Show HN: [Product Name] – [Plain English description of what it does]

[Opening paragraph — what it is, one sentence]

I built this because [personal problem or observation]. [1-2 sentences on why existing solutions didn't work for you].

[Technical approach — what makes this interesting technically. 2-3 sentences. Be specific.]

Current state: [honest assessment — MVP / beta / launched / has X users / generates $Y MRR]

What I'm looking for: [beta users / technical feedback / thoughts on approach]

Tech stack: [brief list]
Pricing: [honest — free/freemium/$X/mo]

[Product URL]

Happy to answer any questions about the technical decisions.
```

**What NOT to do on HN:**
- Don't say "disrupting" or "revolutionary"
- Don't list VC buzzwords
- Don't ask for upvotes
- Don't post "also check us out on PH!" as your main CTA (mention it briefly at most)

**Do respond to every comment within 30 minutes for the first 4 hours.**

## INDIE HACKERS POST

IH loves transparency. Revenue numbers, honest struggles, real lessons.

```
Title: I just launched [PRODUCT] — here's what happened in the first [24h/week]

[Product] helps [target user] [do specific thing]. I've been building it for [X weeks/months].

**The launch:**
- Product Hunt: [N] upvotes, ranked #[X]
- Hacker News: [N] points (if applicable)
- Signups day 1: [N]
- Paying customers so far: [N] (be honest — even if 0)
- MRR: $[X]

**What worked:**
[2-3 bullet points of what actually drove results]

**What didn't work:**
[1-2 honest misses — IH respects this]

**What I'd do differently:**
[1-2 lessons]

Would love feedback from anyone who tried it. The product is [URL] and it's [pricing].

What questions do you have?
```

## RESPONSE TEMPLATES

Megaphone pre-drafts these for launch day. Ghost executes the responses.

**To "How is this different from [Competitor]?":**
"Great question. [Competitor] does [X] well. Our focus is specifically on [specific differentiation]. If [use case], we're probably a better fit. If [other use case], [Competitor] might serve you better. Happy to dig in more."

**To "What's the pricing?":**
"[Honest pricing breakdown]. We have [free tier description] free forever. Paid starts at $[X]/mo for [what you get]. Happy to help you figure out which plan fits."

**To "I've been waiting for something like this":**
"That genuinely means a lot — this is exactly who we built it for. Would love to hear what you've been using in the meantime. Your feedback on what's missing will shape what we build next."

**To critical feedback:**
"Thank you for this — this is the feedback that actually makes the product better. [Address the specific criticism directly.] [What you're doing / planning to do about it.] Seriously, this kind of comment is gold."

**To "I tried it and got [error/bug]":**
"I'm so sorry about that — this should not have happened. I've noted the issue. Can you [specific troubleshooting step or email address]? I want to make this right personally."

## MILESTONE POST

Post this 1-2 weeks after launch. Often drives more signups than launch day.

```
Title: We launched [PRODUCT] [X days] ago — here's the full breakdown

[1 sentence: what the product is]

**The numbers:**
- Total signups: [N]
- Paying customers: [N]  
- MRR: $[X]
- Churn so far: [N or 0]
- Product Hunt final rank: #[X] of the day

**What drove the most signups:**
1. [Source 1] → [N signups] — [why it worked]
2. [Source 2] → [N signups] — [why it worked]
3. [Source 3] → [N signups] — [why it worked]

**The unexpected things:**
[1-2 things that surprised you — positive or negative]

**What I'm focused on now:**
[2-3 specific next steps — be concrete]

**The honest take:**
[1 paragraph — is this working? what's the real trajectory?]

If you haven't tried [PRODUCT] yet: [URL]

Happy to answer anything.
```
