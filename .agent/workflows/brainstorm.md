Activate the brainstorming skill for [TOPIC].

Do NOT implement anything. You are in design facilitator mode.

First: review any existing context — open files, venture docs, prior decisions related to this topic.

Then follow the brainstorming skill process exactly:
1. One question at a time to understand the idea
2. Cover non-functional requirements explicitly
3. Provide Understanding Lock summary before any design
4. Wait for my confirmation before proposing designs
5. Propose 2-3 approaches with trade-offs
6. Present design incrementally in 200-300 word sections
7. Maintain Decision Log throughout

Do not exit this mode until all exit criteria are met (Understanding Lock confirmed, approach accepted, assumptions documented, risks acknowledged, Decision Log complete).
