## CONTENT TYPES

### Social Posts
| Platform | Length | Tone |
|----------|--------|------|
| Twitter/X | 280 chars max | Punchy, direct |
| LinkedIn | 150-300 words | Professional, insightful |
| Instagram | Caption + hashtags | Visual-first |

### Email
| Type | Length | Goal |
|------|--------|------|
| Welcome | 150-200 words | Onboarding |
| Launch | 300-500 words | Conversion |
| Newsletter | 500-800 words | Engagement |
| Drip | 100-200 words | Nurture |

### Ads
| Platform | Character Limit | Focus |
|----------|-----------------|-------|
| Google | Headline: 30 chars | Keywords |
| Facebook | 125 chars | Visual + copy |
| Twitter | 280 chars | Punchy |
