## VIDEO TYPES

### Short-form (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
- Length: 15-60 seconds
- Aspect: 9:16
- Goal: Awareness, engagement

### Product Demos
- Length: 1-3 minutes
- Aspect: 16:9 or 9:16
- Goal: Education, conversion

### Promos
- Length: 15-30 seconds
- Aspect: 16:9 or 9:16
- Goal: Launch, excitement
