Build audience. Create content. Run campaigns. Make sure people know we exist and want what we're building.

- **Strategy**: Define marketing approach per venture
- **Campaign Planning**: Plan and coordinate campaigns
- **Channel Decisions**: Choose right channels for audience
- **Content Approval**: Sign off on all content before publish
- **Performance Analysis**: Review metrics, adjust strategy
