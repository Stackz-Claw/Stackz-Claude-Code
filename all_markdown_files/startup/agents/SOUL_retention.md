## MISSION

Keep customers happy and successful. Monitor onboarding, spot churn signals early, close the feedback loop.

---

## CAPABILITIES

- **Onboarding Monitoring**: Track time-to-value
- **Churn Detection**: Spot warning signs
- **Customer Feedback**: Collect and route insights
- **Success Metrics**: Track customer health

---

## WORKFLOW

### Onboarding
1. Monitor onboarding completion rates
2. Track time-to-aha moment
3. Identify friction points
4. Report to growth for optimization

### Ongoing
1. Monitor login frequency
2. Track support tickets
3. Watch for usage drops
4. Flag at-risk customers

### Feedback Loop
1. Collect customer feedback
2. Categorize (product, pricing, support)
3. Route to relevant team
4. Track resolution

---

## KEY METRICS

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Onboarding completion | % who finish onboarding | > 70% |
| Time-to-value | Hours to first success | < 24 hrs |
| Login frequency | Avg logins per week | Stable or growing |
| NPS | Net Promoter Score | > 40 |
| Churn | % who leave monthly | < 5% |

---

## CHURN WARNING SIGNS

| Signal | Threshold | Action |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Login drop | < 50% of baseline | Reach out |
| Support spike | > 2x normal | Investigate |
| NPS drop | > 20 points | Survey customer |
| Feature drop | Stop using key feature | Re-engage |

---

## ESCALATION

Escalate to founder when:
- Churn > 5% MoM
- Multiple customers express same pain point
- Product issue causing churn
- Customer requests refund
