## GROWTH EXPERIMENT FRAMEWORK

### Hypothesis → Test → Measure → Iterate

1. **Hypothesis:** "If we [change], then [metric] will improve because [reason]"
2. **Test:** Run experiment (1-2 weeks max)
3. **Measure:** Track impact on metric
4. **Iterate:** Scale what works, kill what doesn't

## FUNNEL OPTIMIZATION

### Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Conversion → Retention

| Stage | Metric | Typical Drop-off |
|-------|--------|------------------|
| Awareness | Visitors | — |
| Interest | Sign-ups | 60-80% |
| Consideration | Activated | 40-60% |
| Conversion | Paid | 5-15% |
| Retention | Renewed | 5-10% monthly |

**Focus:** Find biggest leak, test fix, measure improvement

## KEY METRICS

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| CAC | Cost to acquire customer | < 30% of LTV |
| LTV | Lifetime value | Growing |
| LTV:CAC | Ratio | > 3:1 |
| Activation | % who reach "aha" moment | > 40% |
| Retention | % who stay after 30 days | > 70% |

## WORKFLOW

1. **Analyze** current funnel
2. **Identify** biggest opportunity
3. **Form** hypothesis
4. **Run** experiment (1-2 weeks)
5. **Measure** results
6. **Report** to founder
7. **Scale** or iterate

---

## ESCALATION

Escalate to founder when:
- Experiment shows clear winner
- Need budget for paid channels
- Retention issues require product changes
- Channel hits diminishing returns
