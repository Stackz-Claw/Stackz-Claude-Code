# GRADUATION CRITERIA
*When a venture graduates from Startup Team to Stability Team*

---

## OVERVIEW

A venture graduates when it's no longer a "project that might become a business" — it's a business that can run itself. These criteria define when that transition happens.

---

## MANDATORY CRITERIA

A venture MUST meet ALL of these to graduate:

### Financial Viability

| Metric | Minimum | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| MRR | $X/month | Sustained for 3+ months |
| MoM Growth | Positive | At least 2 of last 3 months |
| Gross Margin | > 40% | After direct costs |

### Customer Stability

| Metric | Maximum | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Monthly Churn | < 8% | For 3+ months |
| Customer Concentration | < 30% | No single customer dominates |

### Operational Stability

| Metric | Requirement | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Support Burden | < 8 hrs/week | Not founder-dependent |
| Uptime | > 99% | No incidents last 30 days |
| Process Documentation | Complete | Runbooks, etc. |

---

## STRONG PREFERENCE CRITERIA

These aren't mandatory but strongly preferred:

### Moat Established
- Has at least one defensible moat (see MOAT_FRAMEWORK.md)
- Clear competitive advantage identified

### Team Readiness
- Stability Team has bandwidth
- Guardian can monitor remotely

---

## GRADUATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Self-Assessment
Founder reviews metrics against criteria.

### Step 2: Documentation
- Update VENTURE_BRIEF.md
- Complete STABILITY_LOG.md
- Prepare MOAT_STATUS.md

### Step 3: Proposal
Submit graduation proposal to Stackz:
- Metrics summary
- Moat assessment
- Risk factors
- Recommendation

### Step 4: Approval
Stackz approves or requests changes.

### Step 5: Handoff
- Transfer to Stability Team
- Brief Sentinel
- Close Startup Team tasks

---

## IF NOT GRADUATING

If criteria not met, founder recommends:
- **Continue**: Keep building, address gaps
- **Pivot**: Change approach, new hypothesis
- **Wind down**: Not viable, clean close

---

## RE-ENGAGEMENT

If a graduated venture falls below criteria for 2+ months:
1. Stability Team assesses
2. Recommends to Stackz
3. May re-engage Startup Team for growth sprint
