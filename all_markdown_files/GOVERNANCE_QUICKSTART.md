# Governance Quick-Start Deck (Bicameral Mind)

---

## Slide 1: The Bicameral Mind Architecture

- **Objective:** Transition from "Business Engine" to "Total Life Engine."
- **Core Heads:**
  - **Smoke:** Chief of Operations (COO) — Internal/Personal domain. Health, calendar, cognitive load, personal logistics.
  - **Stackz:** Chief of Business Operations (CBO) — External/Business domain. Revenue, strategy, marketing, development.
- **Key Insight:** Both nodes share a `CORE_CONTEXT.json` for live sync. Governance ensures the business doesn't burn out the owner.

---

## Slide 2: Governance & The Validation Gate

- **Head Nodes:** Smoke (Internal) and Stackz (External) are peer nodes.
- **Governance Logic:** Ghost acts as the Legal Validation Gate.
  - No code deploys, no startup launches, no legal exposure without Ghost sign-off.
  - Precedent validation, drafting/filing, and vaulting legal docs.
- **The Rule:** Strategic decisions pass through Ghost first, then Smoke/Stackz negotiate scheduling, then Stackz approves for execution.

---

## Slide 3: Inter-Node Protocol & Overrides

- **Sheldon Override:** Smoke moves business meetings that conflict with protected personal health/time blocks.
- **The Review Loop:** Stackz requests review; Smoke finds the optimal low-cognitive-load window in the owner's schedule.
- **Logging:** Every override and negotiation is logged to `memory/sync-log.md`.
- **Priority Sequence:** SMOKE (Biological Safety) > STACKZ (Business Operations) > ALL AGENTS.

---

## Slide 4: Knowledge Management (The Ahrens Vault)

- **Philosophy:** From "doing chores" to "developing philosophy."
- **Note Structure (Zettelkasten):**
  - 00_Inbox (Fleeting notes — Smoke captures)
  - 10_Sources (Literature notes — Stackz summarizes)
  - 20_Notes (Permanent atomic notes — Linked and synthesized)
  - 30_Index (Maps of Content — The Knowledge Skeleton)
- **Goal:** Every note must have YAML frontmatter and at least two internal links with "Reason for Link."

---

## Slide 5: What This Means For You

- **Owner:** Two dedicated head nodes working in tandem — one protects your body, one grows your business.
- **Teams:** All cross-team routing still flows through the Lane Queue. Ghost gates all launches.
- **Knowledge:** Every insight, recipe, health pattern, and business lesson becomes a permanent, linked note in the Ahrens Vault.
- **Priority:** If your health is compromised, Smoke overrides everything. No exceptions.
