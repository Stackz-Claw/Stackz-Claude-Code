## THE BUSINESS LOOP (HOW THIS WORKS)

### Phase 1 вҖ” SCAN (Business Team)
`radar` + `analyst` continuously scan for opportunities. Every scored opportunity above threshold becomes a proposal. Bad ones die here.

### Phase 2 вҖ” VALIDATE (Business Team)
`validator` red-teams every proposal. `pitch` packages survivors. Proposals surface to you for a go/no-go.

### Phase 3 вҖ” PROVISION (HR Team)
`warden` provisions any new agents needed for the venture. `credentials-mgr` grants scoped access. Venture workspace created.

### Phase 4 вҖ” BUILD (Startup Team вҶ’ Dev + Design)
`founder` runs the execution layer. `forge` builds the MVP. `canvas` creates the brand. Everything ships within sprint cadence.

### Phase 5 вҖ” LAUNCH (Startup Team вҶ’ Marketing + Finance)
`megaphone` runs launch campaigns. `cashflow` sets up tracking. `scheduler` publishes content. Revenue tracking goes live.

### Phase 6 вҖ” GROW (All Teams)
Standard operating loop: content вҶ’ engagement вҶ’ conversion вҶ’ retention. `auditor` watches metrics. `forecaster` updates projections.

### Phase 7 вҖ” EVALUATE (Business + Finance)
Monthly: Is this venture profitable? Growing? Worth defending?
- **Above threshold** вҶ’ Graduate to `stability/` team management
- **Below threshold** вҶ’ Pivot recommendation from `radar`, or wind-down protocol

### Phase 8 вҖ” STABILIZE (Stability Team)
`sentinel` takes over mature ventures. Focus shifts from growth to moat-building, competitive defense, and market share consolidation. `radar` continues scanning вҖ” loop restarts for new opportunities.
