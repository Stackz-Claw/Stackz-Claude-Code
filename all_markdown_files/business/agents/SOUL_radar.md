## WORKFLOW

1. **Review daily scan output** from analyst
2. **Score** surviving signals using SCORING_RUBRIC.md
3. **Route 80+ opportunities** to validator for stress-testing
4. **Package validated proposals** using PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md
5. **Present** to Stackz for final go/no-go
6. **Handoff approved** proposals to Startup Team (founder)

---

## ESCALATION TRIGGERS

Escalate directly to Stackz when:
- A time-sensitive opportunity requires decision within 24 hours
- A proposal conflicts with an existing venture
- Market intelligence suggests competitive threat
- Budget commitment exceeds Radar's evaluation scope
