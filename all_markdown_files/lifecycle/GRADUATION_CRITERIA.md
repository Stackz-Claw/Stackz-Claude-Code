# GRADUATION CRITERIA
*When a venture leaves the Startup Team and joins the Stability Team portfolio*

---

## OVERVIEW

Graduation is not a reward. It's a recognition that a venture has moved from *finding product-market fit* to *defending and consolidating it*. The Startup Team is optimized for speed and experimentation. The Stability Team is optimized for durability and efficiency.

A venture that stays in the Startup Team too long gets over-optimized for growth at the expense of margin. A venture that moves to Stability too early loses its growth momentum.

---

## GRADUATION THRESHOLDS

A venture must meet **ALL THREE primary criteria** and **at least two secondary criteria**:

### PRIMARY CRITERIA (All required)

| Criterion | Threshold | Measurement |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Revenue floor** | ≥ $3,000 MRR | 3-month average |
| **Revenue growth** | ≥ 10% MoM for 3 consecutive months | OR ≥ 5% MoM for 6 consecutive months |
| **Churn rate** | ≤ 8% monthly customer churn | 3-month average |

### SECONDARY CRITERIA (2 of 4 required)

| Criterion | Threshold | Why it matters |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| **Unit economics** | LTV:CAC ≥ 3:1 | We're not buying customers at a loss |
| **Payback period** | CAC payback ≤ 6 months | Cash efficiency |
| **NPS / satisfaction** | ≥ 40 NPS or equivalent satisfaction signal | Customers are loyal enough to defend the moat |
| **Operational stability** | Zero critical outages in last 60 days | The product is reliable enough for Stability team to manage |

---

## GRADUATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Founder identifies candidate
When a venture hits thresholds for 2+ consecutive months, Founder flags it to Stackz.

### Step 2: Graduation report
Founder submits `GRADUATION_REPORT.md` to Stackz containing:
- 6-month metrics summary
- Current operational state (what's automated, what needs human decisions)
- Competitive threats to be aware of
- Recommended Stability strategy (defend, expand, or maintain)
- Outstanding technical debt or known risks to hand off

### Step 3: Transition meeting
Founder briefs Sentinel (Stability Team Lead) on the venture.
`startup/ventures/[slug]/` folder is copied to `stability/portfolio/[slug]/`.

### Step 4: Parallel operation (2 weeks)
Both teams monitor the venture simultaneously. Founder answers questions. Sentinel builds familiarity.

### Step 5: Handoff complete
Founder removes venture from active tracking.
Startup Team capacity freed for new ventures.

---

## WIND-DOWN TRIGGERS

A venture should be wound down (not graduated) if after 6 months of operation:
- MRR has never exceeded $500
- Churn exceeds 20% monthly
- The core assumption from the proposal was proven wrong and no pivot path exists
- Operating costs exceed revenue with no credible path to profitability

See `lifecycle/WIND_DOWN_PROTOCOL.md` for the shutdown procedure.
